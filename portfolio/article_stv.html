STV Energy Services - Pipeline Project

One of my first projects with STV Energy Services (my employer) involved work on a 160-mile long pipeline that straddles both Ohio and Pennsylvania. The work involved a great deal of asset management - parcels, wetlands, right-of-way records, property plats, and pipeline alignments. In the beginning of the project, two CAD designers were trying to manage some of this data in seven different spreadsheets. I helped to streamline and consolidate the data into one database, initially with Microsoft Access and ultimately with MySQL. Additionally, I would eventually construct a series of web maps, which pulled geographic and tabular data from the backend database. Thus, STV project managers and the client could verify in real-time the status of parcel agreements, the location of completed pipeline segments, and pipeline stationing details.
