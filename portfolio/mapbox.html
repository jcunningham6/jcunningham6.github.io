St. Paul's Rome and his Journeys

I love my faith and I love making maps. So I often look for opportunities to meld those loves together. This particular map is a thematic one called 'St. Paul's Rome and his Journeys', based on Paul of Tarsus, a Christian apostle whose ministry was situated in the middle of the first century A.D. His mission began in Judea and was carried out in various parts of the Roman Empire. I was inspired to develop this particular theme by a viewing of the 2018 film, 'Paul, Apostle of Christ'.
