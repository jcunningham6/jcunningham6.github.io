Boston to the Bayou

As part of a project for my UW-GIS Geog 572 course, I produced a proposed bike race route overlain on a terrain map. Though the route was hypothetical, I wanted segments of that route to include some scenery near and dear to my heart - namely, the view offered in God's country by way of Skyline Drive and the Blue Ridge Parkway in the Shenandoah Valley. This effort involved DEM and land cover geoprocessing in ArcGIS, raster processing with Adobe Photoshop, and vector/raster manipulation in Adobe Illustrator (my first foray ever into both Photoshop and Illustrator).
